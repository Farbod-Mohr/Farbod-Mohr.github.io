Ever gone on a trip or vacation and had to leave your greenery at the hands of someone else, constantly having to worry about whether they’ll be fine when you return? Or even worse, having to leave them out to dry and simply have to hope for the best? We are here to solve that problem.

Our professionals are ready to provide your foliage with the utmost care at budget-friendly prices!

WHAT WE DO

We've got everything your plants need to simply flourish

Brand Identity

Do not fret; your plants will be in vine hands! Our organisation was founded by a few, ever passionate botany enthusiasts. We don't work for profit — we work for you and the love we hold for all nature. Any flora that are important to you, will be to us as well!

Quality

We will stop at nothing to give your potted pals the absolute perfect amount of water, nutrients, light, and even provide them with top-notch pest control. We even have specialized packages for private greenhouse spaces to protect even the most delicate of plants. We are rooting for your greenery!

Marketing

You can find daily blogs made by our team on all of our social media platforms that detail exactly how we treat your precious potted pals! Want to participate in growing this family of plant lovers? Send us videos of you and your plants with #Litu!

Values

We truly and only look for people who are down to earth. We offer employee benefits such as medical, dental, and vision insurance, along with all daycare plans being completely free for all of our team members! We are all one big family of botanists — so we make sure everyone will thrive, without exception.
